Junhong Shen

Undergraduate in Math. of Comp.

University of California, Los Angeles

I’m a fourth-year undergraduate student at UCLA, majoring in Mathematics of Computation. Currently, I study sample-efficient reinforcement learning (RL) under the supervision of Dr. Lin Yang. I also work as a research assitant at Center for Vision, Cognition, Learning, and Autonomy (VCLA), advised by Professor Song-Chun Zhu and Ph.D. candidate Luyao Yuan.

I’m broadly interested in how machine can acquire greater learning ability with insights from human cognitive and communicatiVE skills. In my previous research, I have studied theoretical and practical aspects of reinforcement learning, machine teaching, and multi-agent collaboration. In the near future, I would like to explore more about:

  • develop new learning frameworks with theoretical support;
  • improve the generalizability and efficiency of RL algorithms with optimization/modeling techniques;
  • apply machine learning techniques to real-life problems such as robot control.
